Inspiration

  • The use of technology and digital media consumes our anima(or vitality): our ability to perceive the world through our own senses, as we rely so heavily on cameras to see and headphones to listen, instead of directly engaging in the world around us

  • The game is inspired by “Contract Anima,” prompting the player to use our physical senses(light sensor and the touch sensor) to combat all the digital information that our phone and devices are feeding us
